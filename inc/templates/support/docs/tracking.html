You may track conversions (actions) for your campaign reports. To do this, you must setup a post back link to notify our servers an action has happened.

http://dsp.smrtb.com/bid/converted/

Replace in your postback with the impression id from your tracking token.
Your actual token for this in the postback url will be different depending on what platform you use. If you use HasOffers for example, the token would br {aff_sub}, so your link would look like
http://dsp.smrtb.com/bid/converted/{aff_sub}
