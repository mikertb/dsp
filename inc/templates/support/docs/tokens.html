Tracking tokens may be passed in your destination URL, and will be dynamically replaced with the token value.

{impid} Unique impression ID. We use this in the postback to track conversions.
{carrier} The mobile network operator. Example: Verizon or Vodacom.
{conntype} Wireless connection type, such as 3G, or wireless
{adsize} Dimensions of the associated ad or creative
{make} The manufacturer of the device. Example: Samsung or Apple.
{model} The specific model of the device. Example: iPhone 6+ or Galaxy S6.
{os} The operating system of the device. Example: iOS or Android.
{osver} The operating system version installed on the device. Example: iOS 8.2 or Android 5.0.
{freqdep} The amount of times the user has been shown this ad.
{sessiondep} The number of pages or screens the user has viewed while browsing the publisher's site or app.
{gender} The gender of the device user.
{age} The approximate age of the device user.
{pubid} Local unique identifier for a specific publisher.
{pubname} The publisher's name.
{pubtype} Publisher type (App or Site)
{country} The country of the device user.
{exchange} Exchange the request came from
{linkname} The reference name of the destination link in the campaign settings.
{adname} The reference name of the ad in the campaign settings.
{devid} Hand held device ID
