An exchange is a source of ad inventory comprised of many individual apps or sites. Each exchange may have different types of traffic - be it mostly web based, or app. They may focus on different geographies or specific types of publishers. Each exchange has their strengths, and knowing the details can help better plan campaigns for success.

Smaato is one of the largest mobile specific exchanges. Traffic split is roughly 50% mobile web and 50% mobile app. Have a high volume of inventory globally and can perform well for both content and app install campaigns. Nearly every device OS can be found here.

GumGum is a relatively new and smaller exchange that is desktop-first. All of their inventory currently is website based and categorized as 'premium'. As a newer exchange, there may be less competition here for great potential. Android, iOS, and some feature traffic will be popular here.
Mobfox is a smaller exchange with great traffic for both mobile app and web. Their inventory is high quality. Most devices can be found here.
Tapsense is a medium-high volume exchange. They focus on interstitial (full screen) ad units, which generally perform better than standard banners. Inventory for both Android and iOS.
